About English Conmigo

Our story, mission, and what makes us different.

Our Story

English Conmigo started as private 1-to-1 lessons with our founder, Ali A., and grew into a small team academy with the same mission: make English learning enjoyable, practical, and confidence-building.

Ali holds a Cambridge CELTA qualification (2013) and has taught students internationally, including in Colombia and Japan. After experiencing traditional grammar-heavy learning himself, he built English Conmigo around a more effective approach: consistent practice, real conversation, and a supportive environment where students feel comfortable speaking.

Today, English Conmigo connects learners with teachers who share this philosophy - helping students speak more naturally, feel more confident, and enjoy the journey to fluency.

Our Mission & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To help English learners worldwide speak confidently and naturally through enjoyable, conversation-focused lessons.

💜

Our Approach

We believe in creating a safe, friendly, low-pressure environment where making mistakes is part of learning.

💡

Our Philosophy

You learn faster when you enjoy the process. That's why our lessons are built around real conversation, not boring drills.

What Sets Us Apart

Holistic Approach

Our founder brings experience in meditation, yoga, and personal development. This means we understand that learning English isn't just about grammar - it's about building confidence, managing anxiety, and developing a positive mindset.

Technology-Forward

We leverage modern AI tools and technology to help students learn faster, while never losing the human warmth, personal touch, and real conversation practice that only real teachers can provide.

Boutique Experience

We're not a huge faceless company. English Conmigo is a small, carefully curated team of qualified teachers who genuinely care about each student's progress.

Global Experience

Our teachers have lived and taught around the world, giving them unique insights into different cultures and the specific challenges that Arabic and Spanish speakers face when learning English.

Meet the Founder

Ali A. - Founder & Director

Ali is Cambridge CELTA qualified (2013) with over 10 years of international teaching experience. He has taught students in Colombia, Japan, and online from around the world.

Beyond teaching, Ali has a background in meditation, yoga instruction, and business consultancy. This unique combination allows him to help students not just with their English, but with the confidence and mindset needed to speak without fear.

Ali also works in AI consulting, which keeps English Conmigo at the forefront of modern learning tools and technology - while always maintaining the human connection that makes great teaching.
