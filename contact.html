Common Questions

How do lessons work?

Lessons are conducted via video call (Zoom or Google Meet). Each lesson is 55 minutes and tailored to your goals.

What do I need to start?

Just a computer or tablet with a camera and microphone, plus a stable internet connection. We'll handle the rest!

Can I change teachers?

Yes, you can switch teachers at any time if you'd like to try a different teaching style. Just let us know.
