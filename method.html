Why Choose Us

Our conversation-first method helps you speak naturally and build real confidence.

Our Method

We focus on what helps you speak naturally - not just what helps you memorize rules. Our lessons are conversation-first, practical, and designed to build real confidence.

You'll practice consistently with a teacher who guides you, corrects you kindly, and helps you sound more natural over time. Most importantly: we keep learning enjoyable - when you look forward to lessons, you progress faster.

What Makes English Conmigo Different

💬

Conversation-First

You'll speak more in every lesson. We believe that the best way to learn a language is by using it, not just studying rules about it. From day one, you'll be having real conversations.

🌟

Confidence-Focused

Learn without pressure or judgement. Making mistakes is part of learning, and our teachers create a safe space where you feel comfortable trying, failing, and improving.

📅

Flexible

Schedule lessons around your life. We understand that you have other commitments, so we make it easy to book lessons that work with your schedule.

💻

Modern Support

Helpful resources between lessons, including AI tools where useful. We leverage technology to enhance your learning while keeping the human connection at the center.

How It Works

Your journey to confident English speaking in four simple steps.

1

Book Your Free Consultation

Start with a free 15-minute consultation where we'll discuss your goals, assess your current level, and help you choose the right teacher. No pressure, no commitment - just a friendly chat to see if we're the right fit for you.

2

Get Matched with Your Teacher

Based on your goals, learning style, and schedule, we'll recommend the perfect teacher for you. Whether you need business English with Ali, creative lessons with Anna, or energetic conversations with James - we'll find your match.

3

Start Your Lessons

Book your lessons at times that suit you. Each 55-minute session is packed with conversation, practical exercises, and personalised feedback. Your teacher will track your progress and adapt to your needs.

4

Practice & Progress

Between lessons, you'll have access to resources and support to keep improving. The more you practice, the faster you'll progress. Most students notice significant improvements within just a few weeks.
