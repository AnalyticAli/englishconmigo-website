Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for lessons?
We accept payment via Stripe (credit/debit cards). For lesson packages, you'll receive a secure payment link. Single lessons can be paid when booking through Calendly.
What if I need to cancel a lesson?
We understand things come up! Please give us at least 24 hours notice to reschedule without charge. Late cancellations may be charged as the lesson slot was held for you.
Do lesson packages expire?
5-lesson packs should be used within 3 months, and 10-lesson packs within 6 months. If you need an extension, just let us know - we're flexible!
Can I try before I buy?
Yes! We offer a free 15-minute consultation to discuss your goals and see if we're the right fit. No obligation, no pressure.
Do you offer refunds?
Unused lessons in packages can be refunded within 30 days of purchase. After that, packages are non-refundable but can be transferred to another student with our approval.
What's included in a lesson?
Each lesson includes 55 minutes of personalised instruction, conversation practice, feedback on your English, and follow-up materials or notes as needed.
