Ali A.

Founder & Director

Cambridge CELTA Qualified (2013) | 10+ Years International Teaching Experience

  • Business English
  • Conversational English
  • Confidence Building
  • Adults

Ali specialises in conversational English, business communication, and confidence-building. His teaching style is calm, engaging, and tailored to each student's unique needs and goals.

What sets Ali apart is his holistic approach. With a background in mentorship, personal development, yoga, and meditation teaching, he understands that learning English is about more than just grammar - it's about building the confidence to express yourself naturally.

Ali has taught students from around the world, including in Colombia and Japan, giving him unique insights into the challenges that different language speakers face. He also works in AI consulting, keeping English Conmigo at the cutting edge of modern learning tools.

Best for: Adults seeking business English, professionals wanting to improve their communication skills, and anyone who needs help building speaking confidence.
